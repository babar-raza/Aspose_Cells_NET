Adding Pictures - Aspose.Cells

This online demo describes how to add pictures into the worksheet in a workbook using Aspose.Cells for .NET.

Sometimes, you may need to insert some pictures of your products or your company logo in the worksheet for more readability. Aspose.Cells component gives you the ability to insert pictures of various formats and enhance your worksheet. You can change the height and width of a picture while inserting into the worksheet. You can also put in a picture from stream. You may remove the pictures of the cells in your worksheet too. The demo creates an excel file and inserts two pictures into the first worksheet of the workbook. You can either open the resultant excel file into MS Excel or save directly to your disk.
